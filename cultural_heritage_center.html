Journey Through Time

Explore the rich tapestry of Jharkhand's cultural evolution from ancient civilizations to modern traditions

3000 BCE - 500 CE

Ancient Civilizations

Early tribal settlements, rock art traditions, and the foundation of Austro-Asiatic cultural practices that continue to influence modern Jharkhand.

500 - 1500 CE

Medieval Kingdoms

Rise of tribal kingdoms, development of unique architectural styles, and the flourishing of traditional crafts and metallurgy.

2000 - Present

Cultural Renaissance

Formation of Jharkhand state, revival of tribal languages, and the integration of traditional wisdom with modern technology.

Living Traditions

Meet the vibrant tribal communities that preserve and celebrate Jharkhand's cultural heritage

Largest Community

Santali Heritage

The largest tribal community in Jharkhand, known for their rich oral traditions, Sohrai art, and the beautiful Santali language with its own script - Ol Chiki.

6.2M+ Population
Ancient Wisdom

Ho Traditions

Known for their deep connection with nature, traditional medicine practices, and the vibrant Maghe festival that celebrates the harvest season.

1.2M+ Population
Cultural Keepers

Mundari Culture

Renowned for their traditional governance system, intricate beadwork, and the preservation of ancient agricultural practices and folk songs.

2.3M+ Population
